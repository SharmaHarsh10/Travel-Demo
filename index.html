Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. 15–29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund on hotel and ground arrangements; airline tickets follow the respective airline's policy. We always recommend purchasing travel insurance for added protection.
Absolutely. Groups of 10 or more travellers receive exclusive discounts ranging from 8% to 20% depending on the destination and package. We also offer complimentary upgrades for group leaders and dedicated group coordinators for smooth on-ground management.
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI payments, all major credit and debit cards, and EMI options through select partner banks. Payments can be made in convenient instalments — typically 30% at booking and the balance 30 days before departure.
Travel insurance is not mandatory but is strongly recommended. It covers medical emergencies abroad, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and flight delays. We partner with leading insurers to offer comprehensive plans at competitive rates. Some destinations like Schengen countries require insurance as a visa prerequisite.
Every trip we create is fully customisable. You can choose your preferred hotels, add or remove activities, extend stays at specific cities, add day trips, or request special arrangements like anniversary dinners or private guided tours. Just let our experts know your preferences and we'll build the perfect itinerary.
